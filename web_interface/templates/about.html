About Our Team

ElderSafe Connect is proudly developed by a six-member student team from Swinburne University of Technology. Our team represents a blend of diverse cultural and academic backgrounds, each bringing unique strengths in areas such as software development, cybersecurity, user experience (UX) design, and systems thinking. Despite our varied experiences, we came together with a shared vision—to create a secure, accessible, and reliable platform tailored to the needs of aged-care facilities. This project reflects our collective commitment to solving real-world problems through thoughtful design and collaborative innovation.
