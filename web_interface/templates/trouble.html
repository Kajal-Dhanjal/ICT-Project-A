🔐 Trouble Logging In?

🔑 Resetting Your Password

If you've forgotten your password, use the Password Reset Tool. Enter your registered email, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. This link is valid for 60 minutes. Your new password should be at least 8 characters long and include a combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.

📱 MFA Setup Issues

If you're having trouble setting up or completing Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):

  • Ensure your authenticator app (e.g., Microsoft Authenticator, Google Authenticator) is installed.
  • Use the MFA Setup Page to scan the QR code or manually enter your secret key.
  • If you lost access to your authenticator app, contact IT to reset your MFA setup.
